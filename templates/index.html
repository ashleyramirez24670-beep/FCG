ℹ️ How it works

Step 1: Enter your FraudCat account credentials and test the login

Step 2: (Optional) Specify an email username or leave empty for random generation

Step 3: Click "Start Email Listener" - the system will:

  • Login to your FraudCat account
  • Fetch available domains
  • Create a temporary email address
  • Listen for incoming emails (checks every 20 seconds)
  • Display the full email content when received

Note: The listener will automatically stop after the specified time if no email is received.
